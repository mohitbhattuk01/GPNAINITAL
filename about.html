About Us

Government Polytechnic Nainital, nestled in the scenic hills of Sherwani, Mallital, is one of Uttarakhand's premier technical institutions. Known for its peaceful learning atmosphere and vibrant academic culture, the institute offers diploma programs in Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics, and Information Technology. The campus is enriched with modern laboratories, a resourceful library, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on skill development. Our mission is to shape technically competent and socially responsible individuals who can contribute effectively to the nation's progress. We strive to provide quality education and foster an environment conducive to innovation and overall personality development. Government Polytechnic Nainital is committed to excellence in technical education and aims to be a leading polytechnic in the region.
