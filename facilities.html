4. Pharmacy

6. Mechanical Engineering

Placement Opportunities

Information Technology: Around 85% students are placed in top IT companies like TCS and Infosys.

Electronics: Good placements in electronics manufacturing firms, 75% success rate.

Electrical: Students join power plants and state boards, placement ~80%.

Pharmacy: Around 70% students get placed in hospitals and pharma firms.

Civil: 65% placed in construction projects and consultancies.

Mechanical: 100% placement in mechanical industries and private companies.

Campus Facilities

- Well-equipped laboratories for each department.

- Digital classrooms with smart boards.

- Central library with thousands of books and journals.

- On-campus hostel and sports grounds.

- Free Wi-Fi available throughout campus.
